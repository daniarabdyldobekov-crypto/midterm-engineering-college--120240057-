Admissions

Join Engineering College and start your journey to becoming a world-class engineer.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit academic transcripts and supporting documents.
  3. Attend interview or entrance test (if required).
  4. Receive admission decision via email.
  5. Confirm enrollment and pay tuition fees.
